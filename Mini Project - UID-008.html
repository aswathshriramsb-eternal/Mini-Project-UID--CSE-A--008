🎓 Campus Guide

College Student Hub

Your 4-year blueprint to land a Day 1 placement

Y1
First Year — Foundations
Master C/C++ basics, Data Structures fundamentals, and problem solving. Set up your dev environment and make your first GitHub commit.
Y2
Second Year — Specialization
Choose a niche: Web Dev, Mobile, AI/ML, or Cybersecurity. Join a college tech club, contribute to small projects, and start your LinkedIn profile.
Y3
Third Year — Competition
Competitive Programming (LeetCode/Codeforces), apply for your first internship (target tier-2 to tier-1 companies), and build 2–3 portfolio projects.
Y4
Fourth Year — Final Push
System Design concepts, open source contributions, mock interviews, and campus placement drives. Polish your resume and negotiate your offer.
